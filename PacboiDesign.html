Game Design

Summary

        In my game, you play as pacboi. Your goal is to get to the end of the level by reaching the green square. If you touch a ghost or a wall, you die.

Gameplay

        The goal of the game is to get to the green square at the end of each level. Once you get past all of the levels, you win!! It’s not going to be easy as in every level, your speed increases. The ghosts’ speed will also increase.

Mindset

        Thy shall feel frustrated and self-doubting. But after thou has beaten thy game, thou shalt feel satisfied.

Technical

Screens

  1. Title Screen
  1. Instructions
  1. Game Screen

              a. Show Current Level With Start Button

      b. Game after Start button

  1. Death Screen or You Win!!!!!

Controls

           The player will control Pacboi with the arrow keys. Each time they complete a level, the player will have to press the enter key to move on. A collision will be used to detect if a player touched a wall or a ghost.

Mechanics

        A collision will be used to detect if a player touched a ghost. Also, there will be an Array to detect if Pacboi where the walls are to see if a player touched a wall.

Game Flow

  1. Player starts in the top left corner
  2. Player must navigate with keys around
  3. Player cannot touch walls
  4. Player reaches end of level, next level
  5. Beat all levels:end game
